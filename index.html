About

ELECTROVERT is an International Level Technical Event organised by Electronics Engineering Students’ Association (ELESA), a technical club of Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli, established in the year 1992 and completing its 30 years of excellence this year.

ELESA has 56 board members from different years of Electronics Branch. After a huge gap of 2 years due to the pandemic, we could not conduct this event in offline mode, but now we are extremely overwhelmed to declare to have ELECTROVERT 2022 – 23 in completely offline mode
